The Challenge

A local auto detailing center offering a $600 service needed a cost-effective way to bring in new customers. They had no prior digital advertising experience and relied entirely on foot traffic and word-of-mouth.

My Approach

  • Created a Facebook ad featuring an urgency-driven 50% off coupon offer with scarcity messaging (“11 coupons left”)
  • Built a custom opt-in landing page collecting name, email, and phone for lead capture
  • Ran multiple ad sets at different bid levels to find optimal CPL
  • Structured the funnel to qualify leads before they arrived — only people willing to share contact info moved forward

Results

  • 62 qualified leads on just $425 in ad spend — $6.86 average cost per lead
  • Lowest CPL hit $2.27, proving the funnel concept at very low budgets
  • Scalability model showed: $1,000 spend = ~145 leads, with 10-30% close rates projecting $6,750–$19,800 in revenue
  • Client gained a repeatable, predictable system for new customer acquisition
